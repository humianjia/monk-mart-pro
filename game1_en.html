Monkey Mart

Welcome to Monkey Mart - a fun-filled tropical supermarket world! Here, you will take on the role of a clever monkey boss, leading your monkey team on a business adventure. Imagine your store attracting cute customers: mischievous little monkeys and banana-loving birds, all while you carefully select products and design layouts to entice them to keep coming back. Complete fun tasks, unlock props and decorations, upgrade your store level, and become the star of the market! You can also compete with your friends to see who can stand out in this bustling market. Whether you are a newcomer or a veteran player, Monkey Mart will bring you endless fun and creativity!

Easy and funny gameplay allows you to find joy even in the busiest moments!

A test of intelligence, mastering resource management to become a savvy little businessman and reap great rewards!

Cartoon characters and colorful scenes make every game feel like a fairy tale adventure!
